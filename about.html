Welcome To Our Hotel

Joyville Hotel was born out of the need to alleviate the trouble our people go through all the time to secure a comfortable place for themselves and their visitors in the town. People used to travel to far places at odd hours to get good hotels to chill at. Besides providing comfortable accommodation at reasonable price, Joyville Hotel also prides itself at providing the best kitchen within and around Omanballa area. A taste of any of our delicious meals will leave you with a watering mouth and everlasting memories.

Our kitchen “Paradise Bar” is named after our parent’s renowned restaurant which operated between early 70s to 2000s and still talked about till this day due to their exceptional quality of food; ofe nsalla specifically. Our priority is to satisfy our customers!
